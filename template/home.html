About me


I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Princeton University, working in the Law Combustion Group. I obtained a Ph.D. and a M.A. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Princeton University in 2014 and 2010. My research focus is on the computation, experimentation and modeling of laminar and turbulent premixed combustion. Before coming to Princeton, I have a B.E. in Vehicle Engineering and M.E. in Engineering Thermophysics from the Department of Automotive Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2006 and 2008.

I am a member of the Combustion Institute, the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME), the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA), and the American Physical Society (APS).
